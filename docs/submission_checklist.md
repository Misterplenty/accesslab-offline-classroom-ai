# Kaggle Submission Checklist

Owner-only final tasks:

1. Record the final AccessLab demo video.
2. Upload the video to YouTube.
3. Capture final screenshots from the shot list in `media/README.md`.
4. Add the final YouTube link to `README.md`.
5. Add the final live demo link to `README.md` if one will be provided.
6. Verify the GitHub repository renders cleanly in an incognito/private browser window.
7. Verify `/judge-demo`, `/proofs`, and `/healthz` work in the final environment.
8. Verify all proof links and screenshot links used in the Kaggle form are accessible.
9. Fill the Kaggle submission form.
10. Press submit.

Do not mark the video, live demo, or media gallery complete until the final owner-managed links and screenshots exist.
